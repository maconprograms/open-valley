## Land & Property

**Parcel** - A piece of land with a unique SPAN (School Property Account Number). Warren has 1,823 parcels.

**SPAN** - 13-character identifier for Vermont parcels (e.g., "617-208-10293").

**Dwelling** - A housing unit fit for year-round habitation with separate entrance, sleeping, cooking, and sanitary facilities. One parcel can have multiple dwellings.

**ADU** - Accessory Dwelling Unit. A secondary dwelling on a parcel (apartment above garage, in-law suite).

## Act 73 Classifications

**HOMESTEAD** - Owner's primary residence for 6+ months per year. Requires annual homestead declaration. Lower tax rate.

**NHS_RESIDENTIAL** - Non-Homestead Residential. Second homes, vacation properties, short-term rentals (1-4 units). Higher tax rate.

**NHS_NONRESIDENTIAL** - Non-Homestead Non-Residential. Long-term rentals, 5+ unit buildings, commercial properties. Highest tax rate.

## Occupancy Types

**Full-time residence** - Someone lives here year-round (owner or tenant).

**Second home** - Owner visits occasionally; sits empty otherwise.

**Short-term rental (STR)** - Rented for stays under 30 days (Airbnb, VRBO).

**Long-term rental** - Rented 30+ days at a time, 6+ months per year.

## Data Sources

**Grand List** - Vermont's official property database. Source for parcels, assessed values, homestead status.

**DESCPROP** - Grand List field describing what's on a parcel. "& DWL" indicates a dwelling.

**AirROI** - Commercial service aggregating Airbnb/VRBO listings with coordinates.

**PTTR** - Property Transfer Tax Return. Filed when property changes hands.
